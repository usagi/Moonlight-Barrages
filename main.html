How to Configuration?

Access with URL fragment as your custom settings
http://.../#max_objects=512,frames_per_second=60,enable_rotate=true
[max_objects]
maximum number of objects.
[frames_per_second]
target frame ratio(FPS).
enable_rotate
enable to rotate effect, but it's heavy.
